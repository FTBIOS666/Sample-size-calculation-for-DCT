The folder contains three main items:

1. functions.R: contains all the R function used for sample size calculation for decentralized clinical trials.

2. Figures: contain all the R codes for generating the figures in the paper for the case study and simulation study.
            it contains two sub folders for cross-sectional (independet data) and longitudinal (correlated data) study.

3. Test_distribution: contains all the R codes used for testing the distribution of the constructed weighetd z test staistics 
                      under the null and alternatives for cross-sectional (independet data) and longitudinal (correlated data) study,
                      and for normal or continuous endpoints.
